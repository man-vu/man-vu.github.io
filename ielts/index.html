Everything you need to know about IELTS Pro.

IELTS (International English Language Testing System) is the world's most popular English language test for study, work, and migration. Over 11,000 organizations in 140+ countries accept IELTS scores, including universities, employers, immigration authorities, and professional bodies.

IELTS Pro provides 760+ sample essays with Band 7 and Band 9 examples, 99 speaking topics with model answers, and AI-powered essay scoring. You can browse real exam topics, study high-scoring examples, practice writing under timed conditions, and get instant feedback on your essays.

Our AI scoring engine evaluates your essay across all four IELTS criteria: Task Achievement, Coherence & Cohesion, Lexical Resource, and Grammatical Range & Accuracy. You receive a predicted band score along with detailed feedback and suggestions for improvement.
