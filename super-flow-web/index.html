privacy & security

Your voice never has to leave your machine.

Switch on on-device mode and transcription runs entirely locally with Whisper — no cloud round-trip. Prefer speed? Use our managed cloud, no API key needed. No account required to start, either way.

01

On-device mode

Choose local Whisper and your speech is transcribed on your machine — the audio never touches a server.

02

Privacy Mode

Flip it on and your dictations stay out of history — no history rows, no archived audio, and clipboard or screen context is never used.

03

Managed or your own keys

Cloud speed runs on our managed keys out of the box — no signup for an API key. Prefer your own? Plug them in. Or switch to on-device anytime.

Good to know.

It can stay entirely on your machine: turn on on-device mode and local Whisper transcribes with nothing uploaded, while Privacy Mode keeps dictations out of your saved history. By default Super Flow uses our managed cloud for speed (no API key needed) — switch to on-device, or bring your own keys, anytime.

Yes — in on-device mode. Local Whisper transcribes with no connection. The default cloud mode needs internet, so switch to on-device for fully offline use.

Anywhere there's a text field — Gmail, Word, Google Docs, Slack, Discord, VS Code, Cursor, Notion, Obsidian, Linear, Figma, ChatGPT and more. It types into whatever window has focus.

You choose. Cleanup runs at four levels — from a raw transcript, to light touch-ups, to a standard rewrite, to a full polish that matches your style. Say “backtrack” to retract a sentence on the fly.

Auto-detect handles your language automatically, and on-device Whisper supports dozens — including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Russian and more. Pick a language, or auto, right from the recording overlay.
